The Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resorts has five miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our hiking guides.

Kayaking

Ocean Kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided bird watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
